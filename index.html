Research
Experimental Characterization and Comparison of Three Typical Omnidirectional Mobile Robots
Zhunlun Li, Jiajun Long, Ximan Zhang, Chengyuan Gao, Shixing Jiang, Zheng Zhu, Zhenzhong Jia
International Conference on Advanced Robotics and Mechatronics (ICARM), 2023
Paper / Video

This paper qualitatively summarizes the comprehensive characteristics of three typical omnidirectional mobile robots in indoor scenarios through theoretical analysis and physical experiments. We compare the robot's performance through six representative evaluation metrics (maneuverability, efficiency, etc.) and express the results in a radar chart. The results of this study can assist designers in designing omnidirectional robots more effectively for specific application scenarios.

Research on design and perception of Rhex robots
Ximan Zhang Thesis / Video

Design and manufacture QDD-RHEX robot and QDD-SMALLRHEx robot under QDD collimation drive paradigm. Webots were used to simulate gait and verify the feasibility of the code. Through the single leg verification test (POC), the torque current of the motor, the encoder position and the force array information of the sensor film are measured. Through continuous training, the above data is fitted and calibrated with the truth value of the six-dimensional force sensor. Finally, through the data mapping relationship between the motor and the sensor in the physical test, the stress situation of the legs of the robot walking on the hard ground and the sponge ground is obtained, and the ontology driving and sensing ability of the QDD version of RHex robot is explored.

Ubiquitous Field Transportation Robots with Robust Wheel-Leg Transformable Modules
Haorao Wang, Cunxi Dai, Siyuan Wang Ximan Zhang, Zheng Zhu, Xiaohan Liu, Jianxiang Zhou, Zhengtao Liu, Zhenzhong Jia Paper / Arxiv

This paper presents two field transportation robots with transformable wheel-leg modules, optimized for various challenging terrains, and validated through simulations and field experiments.
